Who We Are

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

In 2010, the non-stock Philippine Technological Institute of Science, Arts, and Trade Inc. was established as a nonprofit, nonsectarian private school to lead the way in technical education. Its eleven founders included academics from both public and private universities; school administrators; managers; engineers; and a scientist/inventor and IT industry practitioner.

Originally established in Tanay, Rizal, the school opened its doors in June 2011. After TESDA accreditation of additional programs, the Trustees' Board decided to open new branches in General Mariano Alvarez, Cavite, and Sta. Rosa, Laguna, for the 2013–2014 academic year.

Today, the GMA Branch proudly serves hundreds of students, offering quality senior high school and college programs in a nurturing, values-centered environment.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Philippine Technological Institute of Sciences, Arts, and Trade, Inc. (PHILTECH) is dedicated to giving quality education to the development of every Filipino. It aims to have students who are value-oriented and physically, academically, socially, and spiritually committed to the achievement of lifelong learning and service to the nation.

Vision

PHILTECH shall emerge as the only technical school in the Philippines that can provide academic excellence and appropriate technology and produce graduates who are globally competitive and shall serve as a pillar in this changing world.

What We Offer

Academic Programs

ASSH – Arts, Social Sciences & Humanities ▾

Targets students interested in human behavior, society, history, communication, and artistic expression, covering subjects like creative writing, journalism, philosophy, political science, sociology, anthropology, and the arts.

ASSH Career paths:

  • Public Administration, Development Studies, International Relations
  • Arts and Design (Visual Arts, Multimedia Arts, Theater Arts)
  • Media and Language (Journalism, Literature, Communication)
  • Psychology (Social Work, Sociology, Anthropology)
  • Journalism, Social Sciences, Education, Government, Research, Arts, Human Resources, and Law
BE – Business & Entrepreneurship ▾

This program is designed for students who are interested in the manufacturing business and want to learn the core principles of the industry. Equips students with essential knowledge and skills needed to succeed in business-related degree programs.

BE Career paths:

  • Accounting and Finance (Accountancy, Management, Economics)
  • Marketing and Entrepreneurship (Business Administration – Marketing Management, Advertising and Public Relations, Digital Marketing)
  • Tourism and Hospitality Management (Hotel and Restaurant Management, Tourism, Business Economics)
  • General Studies (Education, Development Studies, Communication)
ICT – ICT Support & Computer Programming / Creative Arts & Design Technologies ▾

Focuses on concepts and skills in information technology. Teaches programming, website design, animation, and illustration skills, which can help in innovative industries.

ICT Career paths:

  • Computer Engineering
  • Game Designer
  • Graphic Arts
  • Data Encoder
  • Programmer
  • System Analyst
  • Web Developer
  • 3D Animator
  • Layout Artist
HT – Hospitality & Tourism ▾

Emphasizes home economics, food preparation, and wellness. Instructs students in home management, food, nutrition, and health. Prepares people for jobs in food and nutrition, hospitality, and food service, including positions as chefs, bakers, and baristas.

HT Career paths:

  • Hospitality Management
  • Tourism Management
  • Culinary Arts
  • Events and Marketing Coordinator
  • Business Administration and Management
