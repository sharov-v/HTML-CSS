ABOUT

In 2010, the non-stock Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and Trade Inc. was established. Non-profit, non-sectarian private school to lead the way in technical education. Its eleven founders included academics from both public and private universities, school administrators, managers, engineers, and a scientist/inventor and IT industry practitioner. The school is now more widely recognized as PHILTECH.


Originally established at FT., the first school was founded in November of 2010. Catapusan Street in Tanay, Rizal. The Philippine Technological Institute of Science Arts and Trade Inc. opened its doors in June 2011 and began offering two-year programs in business outsourcing management, hotel and restaurant services, and information technology.


With each Board of Trustees member making a special effort to publicize the institution and its program offers, which persisted in the first semester of the 2011–2012 academic year.

MISSION
Philippine Technological Institute of Sciences, Arts and Trade, Inc. (PHILTECH) is dedicated to give quality education to the development of every Filipino. It aims to hone students who are values oriented and physically, academically, socially, and spiritually committed to the achievement of life-long learning and service to the nation.
VISION
PHILTECH shall emerge as the only technical school in the Philippines that can provide academic excellence, appropriate technology and produce graduates who are globally competitive and shall serve as pillar in this changing world.
